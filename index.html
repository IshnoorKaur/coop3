My journey of learning and growth at OPSSC

Introduction

For my 4-month co-op, I worked as an OPSSC Co-op Support Officer with the Ministry of Public and Business Service Delivery, Infrastructure Technology Services Division. My responsibilities included providing front-line IT support to Government of Ontario employees, assisting with hardware, software, and mobile device issues, and delivering both remote and in-person technical services. This role allowed me to strengthen my technical knowledge, customer service skills, and problem-solving abilities in a professional public sector environment.Through these experiences, I deepened my understanding of IT systems and strengthened my ability to collaborate effectively with the team. This also gave me valuable insights into the importance of communication and teamwork in IT support.

Information about the Employer

The Ministry of Public and Business Service Delivery and Procurement, through its Infrastructure Technology Services Division, offers the OPSSC Co-op Support Officer role within the Ontario Public Service Centre. The position provides front-line IT support to Government of Ontario employees, including hardware, software, and mobile device assistance through phone, remote, and in-person services. As part of the GovTechON program, it builds technical expertise and professional skills in a fast-paced public sector environment.

Coop Goals

For my co-op term, my goals and learning outcomes included:


  • CRITICAL & CREATIVE THINKING -Problem Solving

    Goal and Action Plan: Strengthen problem-solving skills by using diagnostic tools, documenting issues to identify patterns, shadowing senior analysts, and reviewing resolved tickets to develop logical and effective troubleshooting approaches.
    Measure of Success : Success will be shown by resolving complex issues more independently, solving problems efficiently, and contributing solutions that reduce repeat issues.
    Benefit for Future Work terms : Improved problem-solving skills will make it easier to handle challenges, work more independently, and add value to future teams.


  • COMMUNICATING - Oral Communication

    Goal & Action Plan : Improve communication of technical information to both technical and non-technical users by practicing active listening, clarifying concerns, engaging in client interactions, and seeking feedback to enhance clarity and confidence.
    Measure of Success : Success will be shown through clear explanations, positive feedback, fewer misunderstandings, and quicker issue resolution.
    Benefit for Future Work terms : Stronger communication skills will support effective collaboration, improve client relationships, and enhance overall professional growth.


  • LITERACY - Technological Literacy

    Goal & Action Plan : Expand technological literacy by gaining hands-on experience with SDA Toolkit, Microsoft 365, Azure, mobile platforms, and diagnostic tools through self-directed exploration, training sessions, and sandbox practice to build deeper understanding and support capabilities.
    Measure of Success : Success will be demonstrated by confidently navigating and supporting core tools, assisting others effectively, and completing tasks efficiently without step-by-step instructions.
    Benefit for Future Work terms : Enhanced technological literacy will provide a competitive edge in future roles by demonstrating adaptability, increasing efficiency in problem-solving, and contributing to team and organizational success.


  • PROFESSIONAL & ETHICAL BEHAVIOUR - Teamwork

    Goal & Action Plan : Strengthen teamwork skills by contributing in team discussions, supporting colleagues during high-volume periods, and fostering collaboration through positive and respectful interactions.
    Measure of Success : Success will be demonstrated when teammates seek input or support, collaboration feels natural, and positive feedback confirms reliability.
    Benefit for Future Work terms : Strong teamwork skills will enhance employability by showing reliability, adaptability in fast-paced environments, and the ability to build productive professional relationships.


  • PERSONAL LEARNING GOAL

    Goal & Action Plan : Build technical and professional skills by setting weekly learning goals, asking questions to deepen understanding, and reflecting on challenges to turn them into growth opportunities.
    Measure of Success : Success will be shown by confidently applying new knowledge, requiring less guidance, and receiving positive feedback on development.
    Benefit for Future Work terms : Continuous learning will make it easier to adapt to new challenges, pick up skills faster, and show initiative that helps in both current and future roles.

Conclusion

In this blog, I have shared my co-op experience at the Ministry of Public and Business Service Delivery, highlighting the development of key skills such as problem-solving, communication, teamwork, and technological literacy. These experiences have enhanced my confidence in supporting others, strengthened my technical foundation, and prepared me to contribute more effectively in future work terms and my career in IT.
